Welcome to Enter the Tower! A game about ascending up a tower as high as you can.

Choose your class!

Warrior

Warriors have a high base damage and high health. Consider this if you want a class with a large health pool.

Elementalist

Elementalists have a low health but they are able to control and manipulate the elements to their desire.

Assassin

Assassins are the glass cannons of this game and can easily take out enemies but be careful because he has a very small health pool.
