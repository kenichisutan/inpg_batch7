Welcome to Enter the Tower! A game about ascending up a tower as high as you can.

Choose your class!

Warrior

Warriors have a high base damage and high health. Consider this if you want the simple class.

Elementalist

Elementalists have a low health but they are able to control and manipulate the elements to their desire.

Assassin

Assassins are the glass cannons of this game and can easily take out single target enemies but be careful when dealing with multiple enemies at once.
